Hi! My name is Camille .

Why that combo? Learning software language was just like learning any another vocal language. You also get to create and collborate on endless projects. The minor was to make sure I didn't lose my French skills. Cause skills need to be maintained. It was also a nice way to meet people outside my major.

This has given me the opportunity and the priviledge to experience cultures other than my own. This has encouraged me to be curious about my surroundings close and far. I believe this helped me fear less of the unknown but I of course have my doubts here and there. It also fuels one of my hobbies, travelling.
